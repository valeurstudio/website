Case Study

Magnitude5

A founder-led wellness brand built from scratch on Shopify as a complete digital platform where Dani Ferreira's personal brand and the Magnitude5 business grow together as one experience.

Industry Wellness
Services Web Development
Brand Direction
Year 2026
Website Magnitude Five Wellness | Official Website ↗

The Client

A coach with eight years of work and a community that was already paying attention.

Dani Ferreira has been coaching for eight years. Her background spans psychology, health science, strength training, corrective exercise, Pilates, yoga, and gut health. Her own experience with eating disorder recovery and competitive athletics shaped a coaching philosophy that credentials alone cannot produce. Structure that supports rather than restricts. Accountability that builds confidence rather than pressure. Movement that people want to keep doing.

Magnitude5 is the community that philosophy became. Built around five pillars covering the full picture of health: physical, mental, emotional, spiritual, and intellectual. Training guides, a wellness journal, custom programming, and a growing presence across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. The brand was already real. The platform was not.

The Problem

A real brand with no real digital foundation.

Magnitude5 had social presence and a genuine audience. But there was no website that matched the level of what Dani was actually building. The need was not simply a site. It was a digital foundation that could introduce Dani as a founder and coach, explain the philosophy behind the brand, present her offers clearly, and create trust with people discovering the brand for the first time.

The site also needed to hold two identities at once. Dani as the personal face of the brand and Magnitude5 as the business. Too personal and it becomes an influencer page. Too corporate and the human connection disappears. Finding that balance without resolving it into something generic was one of the most important parts of the project.

Direction

One platform. Both sides of the brand, together.

We built from scratch on Shopify with a clear goal: create a platform where Dani's personal brand and the Magnitude5 business identity could strengthen each other rather than compete. The visual direction was refined so the site felt cohesive, elevated, and aligned with what the brand actually stands for. Strength, movement, confidence, healing, support, and long-term personal growth.

That tone needed to come through in every section, not just in the palette. Structure, hierarchy, and content flow were all shaped so the site could introduce Dani, present the philosophy, show the offers, and build trust inside one connected experience rather than across disconnected pages.

The Insight

Her name gives the brand its trust. The platform gives the trust a home.

What Magnitude5 needed was a digital foundation that could hold both at once. A place where Dani's expertise and the business could work as one thing instead of two separate stories requiring constant explanation.

Web Design & Development

A conversion-ready platform built around a real customer journey.

The build was structured around how someone actually moves through a brand like this. Landing, learning, trusting, and then taking the next step. Navigation, content flow, calls to action, and how the offers were introduced all needed to support that journey rather than simply present information. We organized the site so coaching, training, programs, digital products, and community could all live in one place without the experience feeling scattered.

The Shopify platform supports both service-based and product-based revenue through one connected experience. Coaching and training alongside digital products and educational resources, all accessible through the same structure. Someone arriving from social or search has a clear path from discovery to action rather than a collection of disconnected links to chase.

Brand Direction

Visual identity aligned with what the brand stands for.

We helped refine the exact colors and overall visual direction so the site felt cohesive, elevated, and aligned with Dani's message. This was not aesthetic work in isolation. It was about defining how Magnitude5 should feel when someone first encounters it. Polished, calm, strong, and intentional, while keeping the founder-led connection intact throughout.

The visual foundation made Magnitude5 feel like a brand with direction rather than a basic page with services listed on it. For a founder-led brand built around coaching and transformation, how the site feels in the first few seconds shapes whether someone trusts what comes next. That first impression had to be right.

The Outcome

A brand home that works on every level.

Magnitude5 launched with a Shopify platform built to function as a personal brand platform, a business platform, an ecommerce destination, and a community home simultaneously. Dani has a central place where her story, expertise, and offers are communicated clearly and professionally.

Visitors arriving from social or search have a clear path into programs, training, products, and community from the first page they land on. The brand feels more human, more trustworthy, and more established because the personal and the professional are no longer competing with each other. They are the same thing.

A unified brand platform

Personal identity and business identity in one place, strengthening each other rather than requiring constant explanation to every new visitor.

Conversion-first structure

Site organized around how someone discovers, trusts, and then acts rather than simply presenting what Magnitude5 offers and leaving the next step unclear.

A scalable digital foundation

A Shopify platform that supports coaching, programs, digital products, and community in one connected experience built to grow with the brand over time.

In Their Words

"Valeur Studio gave Magnitude5 a real home online. Before this, the brand existed across too many places and none of them told the full story. Now someone can land on the site, understand exactly who I am and what this brand stands for, and know where to go next. That clarity made everything feel more credible."

Dani Ferreira Founder, Magnitude5
