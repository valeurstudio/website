What Is Holding the Site Back

The findings below have the biggest impact on how visitors find and respond to the business.

  • ✕Value PropositionVisitors arriving at the site cannot immediately tell what the business does, who it serves, or why they should stay.
  • ✕Page Load SpeedThe site loads slowly on mobile, which is where most visitors are arriving from.
  • ✕Search PreviewThe search preview description is missing on key pages, reducing the chance of earning a click from search results.
  • ✕Call to ActionThere is no clear next step in the first section of the page, so visitors who are ready to act have nowhere obvious to go.
  • ✕Customer FocusThe copy reads more like a list of features than a conversation with the customer.

What Is Working Well

These areas are already solid and do not need attention right now.

  • ✓Secure ConnectionThe site runs on a secure connection, so visitors see no warnings from their browser and their information is protected.
  • ✓Page Load SpeedPages load quickly, with the main content appearing well within the expected range for a good visitor experience.
  • ✓Page HeadlineEach key page opens with a clear main heading so visitors immediately know where they are and what to expect.
  • ✓Mobile PerformanceThe site displays and performs well on mobile devices, where most visitors are arriving from.

Recommended Actions

Ordered by priority. Each one addresses a specific finding from the report above.

01
Headline

Write a homepage headline that immediately tells a first-time visitor what your business does and who it is for.

High
02
Page Load Speed

Compress images and remove files and code that are not being used so the site loads quickly for visitors on all devices.

High
03
Search Preview

Add a search preview description to each key page so your listing in search results accurately represents your offer.

High
04
Call to Action

Add a clear, visible button or link in the first section of every key page so visitors who are ready to act have an obvious path forward.

High
05
Customer Focus

Rewrite your copy to focus on what customers experience and gain, not on the process or features you offer.

Medium
