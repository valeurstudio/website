Process

How we work.

From the first inquiry to delivery and follow-up, every step is built to keep the work clear, considered, and moving in the right direction.

Valeur Studio

Before We Begin

How it starts.

Most clients come to us with a clear need and a less clear idea of where to start. That is fine. We take time at the front of every project to make sure we understand what you are building and whether we are the right fit to help you build it.

01

Inquiry

You reach out through the contact form or run the website audit. We review every submission and respond within 48 hours.

02

Discovery conversation

A focused call to understand your business, your goals, and what success looks like for this project. No pitch, just questions.

03

Proposal

If it is a good fit, we put together a clear scope, timeline, and investment. What you see in the proposal is what you pay.

04

Kickoff

Once the project is confirmed, we schedule a kickoff call and get to work. Every project starts with a shared understanding of what we are building and why.

Brand Identity

How we build your brand.

Brand identity is the most research-intensive of our services. Before anything is designed, we spend time understanding your business, your audience, and the visual territory you need to own. That foundation is what makes the work last.

Brand Identity
01

Discovery

We learn your business, your competitors, and what your brand needs to communicate. This shapes everything that follows.

02

Direction

We present a clear creative direction before any design begins. Agreed direction means no wasted rounds later.

03

Design

Logo, color, typography, and the full identity system, built in alignment with the approved direction. Every element considered, every decision documented.

04

Delivery

Revision rounds, final production files, and a brand guidelines document so the system can be used consistently from day one.

Web Design

How we build your site.

Every site we build starts with structure, not visuals. We need to understand how your customers think and what your business needs to communicate before a single screen is designed. The platform follows that understanding. Whether that means custom development, Shopify, Squarespace, or something else, we choose what fits the project.

Web Design
01

Brief

We define goals, audience, key pages, and the actions you need visitors to take. Structure comes before design.

02

Wireframe

We map the architecture and content flow before any visual decisions are made. This is where the site earns its logic.

03

Design

We build the visual layer in full alignment with your brand, across every screen, every state, every detail considered.

04

Build and launch

Development, QA, and a full handoff. You own the site and know how to run it from day one.

See the full Web Design service →

Product Sourcing

How we bring products to life.

Product sourcing involves more moving parts than most clients expect. Concept, vendor selection, production, and fulfillment all have to be managed tightly for the end result to hold up. We oversee the entire process so the product that arrives matches what was approved.

Product Sourcing
01

Concept

We define what the product is, what it should say, and how it fits into the wider brand before anything is ordered.

02

Sourcing

We identify and vet vendors based on quality, lead times, and fit. You get honest options with clear trade-offs, not just one route.

03

Production

We design the product and its packaging, manage proofing rounds, and oversee manufacturing so what gets made matches what was approved.

04

Delivery

Final quality check, fulfillment coordination, and full production documentation so future reorders come through us cleanly.

See the full Product Sourcing service →

After Delivery

What happens after handoff.

A project closing is not the end of the relationship. We follow up, make sure everything is working, and remain available during the transition period so nothing falls through the cracks.

Files and documentation

Everything is delivered clean, labelled, and organised. No hunting for assets or wondering what version is final.

Web training

For website projects, we walk you through the CMS, admin access, and everything you need to run the site independently from day one.

Launch check-in

We follow up after delivery or launch to catch anything that needs attention before the project fully closes.

Ongoing availability

We remain available for questions in the weeks after handoff. If something is unclear or needs a small adjustment, you can reach us.
